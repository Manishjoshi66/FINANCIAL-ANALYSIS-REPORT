# FINANCIAL-ANALYSIS-REPORT

## The Fest-Man Stores Sales Dashboard is a Power BI project that analyzes retail sales performance across countries, customer segments, and products. It provides interactive visualizations for KPIs, profit margins, discount analysis, monthly sales trends, and top products, helping businesses monitor growth, identify profitable segments, and make data-driven decisions.

#📊 Fest-Man Stores Sales Dashboard – Detailed Description
##📑 Dataset Summary

Rows: 3,900
Columns: 18

Key Features

Customer Information – Includes customer name, segment, and region details to understand purchasing behavior across different customer groups.

Geographical Data – Country, city, and region fields allow analysis of sales distribution across locations.

Product Details – Product name, category, and sub-category help identify which products contribute most to revenue.

Order Information – Order date, ship date, and order ID provide insight into order frequency and shipping timelines.

Sales Metrics – Sales amount, profit, quantity, and discount applied enable profitability and performance analysis.

Data Quality

Missing Values: A small number of missing entries were found in certain fields during data inspection.

Duplicate Checks: Order IDs were verified to ensure data consistency.

Data Formatting: Date columns and numerical columns were standardized to ensure accurate calculations in the dashboard.

#🧹 Data Cleaning & Preparation
Data Loading and Initial Exploration

The dataset was imported into Microsoft Power BI where initial exploration was performed using Power Query. This step helped understand the dataset structure, column types, and overall distribution of data.

Handling Missing Values

Missing values were reviewed and handled appropriately. In cases where missing values were minimal, records were either corrected using available data or excluded from analysis to prevent inaccurate results.

Data Transformation

Several transformations were performed to make the dataset suitable for analysis:

Converted date columns into proper date format

Ensured numeric columns such as Sales, Profit, and Discount were properly formatted

Renamed columns for better readability

Removed unnecessary fields not required for dashboard insights

Data Modeling

Relationships between tables were created (if multiple tables were used) to enable efficient filtering and aggregation across visuals.

# 📈 Dashboard Features
KPI Indicators

The dashboard highlights key performance indicators that provide a quick overview of business performance:

Total Sales

Total Profit

Sales Growth Compared to Previous Year

Total Orders

These metrics help users quickly understand the overall health of the business.

Sales by Country

This visualization shows the number of orders or total sales across different countries, allowing businesses to identify high-performing geographic regions.

Profit by Country

Displays the profit generated in each country, helping identify markets that contribute the most to overall profitability.

Discount Distribution

A donut chart illustrates how sales are distributed across different discount bands (Low, Medium, High). This helps analyze the impact of discount strategies on sales performance.

Profit Margin by Segment

This chart compares profit margins across customer segments such as:

Small Business

Midmarket

Government

Enterprise

Channel Partners

It helps determine which segment provides the highest profitability.

Monthly Sales Trend

A line chart tracks sales performance across months and years. This visualization helps identify seasonal trends and sales growth patterns.

Top Products by Sales

A bar chart highlights the top-performing products based on sales value, helping companies prioritize inventory and marketing efforts.

# 💡 Business Insights

This dashboard enables decision-makers to:

Identify top-performing countries and regions

Understand which customer segments generate the most profit

Evaluate discount strategies and their impact on revenue

Track monthly sales performance and seasonal patterns

Recognize high-demand products driving sales
